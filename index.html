CONCEPT

A Modern Asian cuisine with full of excitements and flavors.

"Hot and spicy Thai food" and "fresh and herby Vietnamese food". We serve you a Modern Asian cuisines which is a perfect mix of these food. Herbs are transported by air from Thailand and Vietnam. All Sauces and seasonings are made fresh in the kithcen using the best ingredients. Please enjoy the bite-size appetizers followed by entrees to share and of course the pho made with homemade pho noodles to finish up the meal.
